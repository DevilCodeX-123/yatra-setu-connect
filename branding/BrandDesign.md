## Typography
- **Font Family**: Poppins
- **Heading**: Bold (Weight 700) - For "Yatra"
- **Sub-heading**: Medium (Weight 500) - For "Setu"
- **Attributes**: Upright, clean, high-readability.

## Logo Concept
The logo is a fusion of a Bridge (Setu) and a Path (Yatra).
1. **The Arch**: A sleek blue arch representing a bridge and connectivity.
2. **The Path**: A negative-space road path cutting through the center.
3. **The Bus**: A minimal capsule shape moving along the path.

## Visual Variations
- **Full Logo**: Icon + Text (Horizontal) - Used for Navbars.
- **Icon Only**: The Bridge-Path symbol - Used for App Icon/Favicon.
- **Monochrome**: Pure white version for dark blue backgrounds.
